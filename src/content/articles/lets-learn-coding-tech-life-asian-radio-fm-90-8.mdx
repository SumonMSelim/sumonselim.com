I (Founder, Lets Learn Coding) along with my friend [Rakibul Hasan Rashed](https://www.facebook.com/rakibulhasan.rashed1) (Faculty, Department of CSE, IUBAT) were special guests on the show **“Lets Learn Coding @ Tech Life”** on **Asian Radio FM 90.8** this Saturday.

We talked about coding/programming and ICT industry of Bangladesh. Discussed about the mission and vision of Lets Learn Coding. You can hear the full recorded version of the program below.

The show was hosted by RJ [Nazib Rafe](https://www.facebook.com/hopperduke).
